## About

Hi! I’m a recent UC Berkeley graduate from the island of [Saipan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saipan,_Northern_Mariana_Islands) in the [Northern Mariana Islands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Northern_Mariana_Islands), and I studied Computer Science and Data Science with an emphasis in cognition. 

This upcoming fall, I'll be attending the [Baskin School of Engineering](https://engineering.ucsc.edu/) at UC Santa Cruz for my Master's in Computer Science, with a research focus in equitable computing education under [Professor Hao Yue](https://campusdirectory.ucsc.edu/cd_detail?uid=hyue11). 

I'm passionate about finding ways to make computing and data science education more equitable and accessible. I discovered my passion for computing education through my position as an undergraduate teaching assistant for [Data 8: Foundations of Data Science](https://www.data8.org/) (UC Berkeley's largest course!), teaching weekly discussion sections across core data science topics using Python and Jupyter notebooks. I'm currently spending my final summer in Berkeley as a Head Teaching Assistant for the course.

I also organize as a proud member of [UAW Local 4811](https://www.uaw4811.org/), the union of 48,000 academic workers at the University of California. In [UC Berkeley EECS and Data Science](https://eecsdsstaff.org/), a supermajority of academic workers are undergrads—but it's only because of member organizing that we've won strong workplace protections in order to maintain the quality of instruction that students deserve.
